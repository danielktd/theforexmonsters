About the Forex Monsters

How we started trading Forex

Forex Monsters' Story

Forex Monsters was founded in 2019 by Wan Patrique and Ahikyirize Daniel with a core aim of extending their skills in the financial markets to young upcoming entrepreneurs.Having been raised from poor backgrounds and making it financially through forex trading after years of struggling, we found it important to extend our knowledge to the everyone we can reach.

We are the first trading and training institute in Uganda. Since then, we have been ranked as the best traders in Uganda, and have taught very many people how to trade the financial markets.

As for me Daniel, got raised up from a poor background in western Uganda, everything in life wasn’t enough at all.
That was my main driving force to achieve in life. Those early days, I started with learning and trying out different internet businesses and jobs.

I tried out very many because that was my only option, tried publishing eBooks on Amazon, and drop shipping, but all that didn’t work out.
It was time to make more research and that’s how I learned of forex trading. This was my greatest opportunity in life, no competition and no conditions, just learning the skill and starting to earn.

I started off with learning on how to trade forex, dedicated all my time to learn and master the skill. It took me too much time because it was self-learning, good months of learning.
I started off my trading career, dropped out of university in my first year to focus on my trading career.

By then forex trading wasn’t popular in the country and that’s when I linked up with Patrique, our goal was to show people what’s possible and extend financial literacy in the forex markets to a bigger audience.

We have tried our best and our goal is manifesting. Just a single quote for you, “keeping working on yourself, in these markets, you first Learn and Earn”.

Ahikyirize Daniel

Expert Mentor, Forex Monsters

Who is Patrique, I was born on 1st-12-2000 to a single mother in a disadvantaged society? I lived with my mother for 14 years until she was exhausted and not able to take care of me anymore so she sent me to my father’s house for academic dues.

Having grown up in a society with humble people and seeing my mother toil to get basic needs for me and my siblings, I understood that I have to work and achieve faster to make a life for my family therefore, all the time I stayed with my father my mind was not on school but financial freedom.

I started as a YouTuber in 2018 but later discovered forex trading and understood that it was exactly what I was looking for. I dedicated in it all my time and efforts then dropped out of senior six and returned back to my mum who was living in a refugee camp at that moment.

When I was there I focused and mastered the skill then began consistently making profits using the school fees that was meant to pay my third term.

By then forex trading wasn’t popular in the country and that’s when I linked up with Daniel our goal was to show people what’s possible and extend financial literacy in the forex markets to a bigger audience. We have tried our best and our goal is manifesting. Just a single quote for you, “keeping working on yourself, in these markets, you first Learn and Earn.

Wani Patrique

Expert Mentor, Forex Monsters
